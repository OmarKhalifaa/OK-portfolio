About

Designing at the intersection of

I'm a senior product designer based in Cairo, Egypt, specialising in design systems, mobile UX, and the infrastructure that makes great products consistent at scale.

At Vodafone I built the Master Design System Kit from the ground up — establishing the token pipeline, component architecture, and the processes that let 40+ product teams ship faster with better consistency.

Outside systems work, I'm drawn to fintech and telecom interfaces where the UX challenge is making genuinely complex domains feel simple: onboarding flows, plan management, identity verification.
